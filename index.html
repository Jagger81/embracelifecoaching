What is Coaching?

At Embrace Life Coaching, I am dedicated to empowering individuals like you to release your inner strength and achieve your wildest dreams.

Coaching is a collaborative process that aims to unlock an individual's potential and maximise their performance in various aspects of life, whether personal or professional. Unlike mentoring or counselling, which often involve providing advice or solutions, coaching focuses on empowering individuals to find their own answers, make informed decisions, and take action towards their goals.

I work closely with my client to facilitate self-discovery, goal setting, and action planning. Through thought-provoking conversations, active listening, and powerful questioning techniques, I will help the client gain clarity, overcome obstacles, and create sustainable change.

Coaching operates on the belief that individuals possess the resources and capabilities needed to achieve their objectives but may require support and guidance to tap into them fully. By fostering self-awareness, accountability, and growth mindset, coaching enables clients to unlock their potential, realise their aspirations, and lead more fulfilling lives.

Overall, coaching serves as a transformative journey that empowers individuals to thrive, both personally and professionally, by leveraging their strengths, values, and aspirations.

Personal and Professional Workshops

These workshops offer invaluable opportunities for individuals to enrich various aspects of their lives, both personally and within the workplace. Tailored to the needs of organisations, these workshops provide essential skills development, heightened self-awareness, and well-being promotion in both personal and professional contexts. By investing in these workshops, organisations can foster a healthier, more engaged, and more productive workforce.

Here's a glimpse of the workshops I offer and their potential benefits:

1 - Building Resilience:

Participants learn strategies to deal with challenges and setbacks, fostering resilience in personal and professional spheres. Workshops help navigate transitions and adapt to change effectively, fostering resilience and growth amidst organisational or personal changes.

Focuses on improving individual productivity, time management, goal setting, and prioritisation skills to enhance personal effectiveness and achievement. Explores the importance of setting healthy boundaries in personal and professional relationships, promoting self-care and well-being.

5 - Work-Life Integration:

Explore strategies for balancing personal and professional responsibilities, promoting well-being and satisfaction in both domains. Workshops raise awareness and provide tools for maintaining and promoting positive mental well-being.

These workshops are designed to empower individuals to thrive personally and professionally, enhancing their overall well-being and contributing to a more harmonious and productive work environment.

Frequently Asked Questions

  • 1. What is Coaching?

    Coaching is a professional relationship designed to assist a client to identify and work towards achieving their personal or business goals or aspirations. It involves partnering with a client in a thought-provoking process that inspires them to maximise their personal and professional potential. (ILI & ICF definitions)

  • 2. Is there a minimum amount of sessions I must sign up for?

    No, coaching is a bespoke process with flexibility aligned to your needs. It allows individuals to tailor their coaching experience to their specific needs and preferences. Some may benefit from ongoing sessions to delve deeper into their goals and challenges, while others may find clarity or resolution after just one session. It's all about empowering clients to take charge of their personal and professional development journey in a way that works best for them.

  • 3. Is there anything I need to do before our session?

    We can have a complementary connection call beforehand to ensure alignment and clarity between coach and client. It allows for setting expectations, discussing logistics, and addressing any initial questions or concerns. I will provide you with my Coaching Agreement which must be reviewed and signed by you before Coaching begins. This helps to establish a clear understanding of the coaching relationship and its parameters.

    I ask clients to consider what they want to bring to the session beforehand to foster readiness and intentionality. This preparation can include reflecting on specific goals, challenges, or areas of focus they wish to explore during the session. However, it's reassuring to know that even if clients are unsure, there's an opportunity at the start of the session to clarify and prioritise their objectives together.

    Overall, this proactive approach ensures a smooth and productive coaching experience, where clients feel prepared and supported from the outset.

  • 4. Will you give me specific advice on how to achieve my goals?

    The approach I follow is non-directive, which means that I don't provide specific advice on how to achieve your goals. Instead, I create a supportive and empowering environment where you take the lead in generating ideas and solutions. It's about fostering self-discovery and self-reliance, where you explore your own challenges, values, and motivations. This approach is designed to empower you to take ownership of your goals and actions, leading to personal and professional growth and development.

  • 5. Will my sessions be confidential?

    Certainly. Maintaining confidentiality is paramount in coaching, and your sessions will be held in a completely confidential and non-judgmental space. This commitment to confidentiality aligns with the International Coaching Federation Code of Ethics, of which I am a member. You can trust that any information shared during our sessions will be kept confidential to ensure your privacy and trust are respected.

  • 6. What is the cost of the sessions?

    The cost of the sessions will be determined based on your specific needs and requirements. Once you reach out to me, I will provide you with a quote tailored to your individual circumstances and goals. Please feel free to contact me to discuss your needs further and receive a personalised quote.
